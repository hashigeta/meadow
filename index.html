The meadow behind my eyelids
Kohei Endo, 2007
11 seconds of video(720x540px) in mp4 format
This is a video work that I shot in 2007.
I found it among a large number of hard drives in a warehouse.
When I was 27 years old, I modified a digital video camera that I bought from a friend to make this piece.
It was an experimental attempt to express the view I saw behind my eyelids.
I destroyed the hard disk that was recording this work.
Therefore, there is only one of this work in the world.
